August 31, 2016

Two Goals in Two Minutes Hand Vikings First Tie

Vikings scored first but Bulldogs answered seconds later.

The Varsity Vikings played an exciting game against the Bulldogs of Butler High School in Mint Hill. The game remained scoreless until the final minutes when Senior Midfielder Alex Tabaczynski scored the first goal. The Bulldogs answered the goal with a goal of their own with only 30 seconds left on the clock. This non-conference game ended in a tie.

August 23, 2016

First Home Game Ends in a W for the Vikings

The Vikings dominate in 6-2 win at home.

The Varsity Vikings looked strong in their win against Central Cabarrus High School. In the end, CCHS was no match for the Vikings of North Meck.
